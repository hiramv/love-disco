Ibiza Guide

OKU IBIZA

Cami del Portixol, 2, 07820 Sant Antoni de Portmany, Illes Balears, Spain

This is where we will be staying and serve as our home base for the weekend! It's a luxury lifestyle hotel that has all the resort amenities our group will need for a fun weekend. There are two large pool areas— one has DJs in the afternoon the other is quieter— and the Cala Gracio public beach is just a short walking trail from the resort. They also have a nice gym and wellness program of yoga classes and sound baths for early risers. We have secured a 15% discount applicable to any rate and any room type for our guests. Book directly on the OKU website with code WEDDING2026.

MONTESOL EXPERIMENTAL

Passeig de Vara de Rey, 2, 07800 Eivissa, Illes Balears, Spain

Located in Ibiza's Old Town, this art deco gem is reimagined with playful, design-forward interiors, a lively bar, restaurant, and an open-air rooftop. With only 33 rooms, designed by Dorothée Meilichzon, this is the epitome of a boutique stay with a local feel. This is where we will host the Welcome Drinks event on Sunday. They are extending a 10% discount off their flexible rates to our guests. Book directly on the website and use code CHELHIRAM.

THE STANDARD IBIZA

Carrer de Bartomeu Vicent Ramon, 9, 07800 Eivissa, Illes Balears, Spain

Set right in the heart of Ibiza Town, The Standard offers minimalist rooms with a buzzing rooftop pool and bar. The ground floor cocktail bar and restaurant has a sexy design that makes for a great start to any night. It's a perfect hotel for those who want to stay where there's always a vibe. We have secured a very competitive rate of 380 Euros per night including breakfast, for a limited number of rooms over the dates of May 23 - 26, 2026. To book, email Ayelen.Priore@standardhotels.com and reference the wedding of Chelsea & Hiram. Conditions: they will charge the first night upfront, and the rest can be paid on arrival. The first night will not be refundable if the reservation is cancelled.
